About the Project

This project uses machine learning to predict obesity levels based on various physical and lifestyle factors. The predictions are made using three different algorithms, each with its own strengths:

  • Logistic Regression: Good for understanding feature importance and probability estimates
  • K-Nearest Neighbors: Works well with non-linear patterns and is intuitive to understand
  • Polynomial Regression: Captures complex relationships between features

Try each algorithm and compare their predictions. The confidence scores help you understand how certain the model is about its predictions.
